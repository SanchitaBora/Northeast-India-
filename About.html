NORTHEAST

Northeast has tonnes of interesting things that one must access and enjoy. It comprises modern days Arunachal pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim, Tripura. Sikkim is traditionally considered as the brother of all other northeastern states.

Northeast is a hub of culture and tradition where a lot of festivals are celebrated and lot of rituals are followed. It is a land where more than 50 tribes are there, each of which has their own culture, festivals and traditions. It is endowed with favourable amount of percipitation for the healthy growth of plants and endemic fruits and animals.

Northeast is blessed with more than 20 dance forms both folk and cultural.Each state have its own dance form. Each have its own food items which have blend of spices and I am sure you will like it. Its smell force the mouth to water. The traditional dresses are indigenous to the people and place which completely vary from one another. The attire makes the peaple look beautiful.
